THERMOL-D

A Multifunctional Combustion Catalyst For DIESEL

THERMOL-D is an eco-fnendly, multifunctional diesel additive developed in association with Indian Institute of Technology (I.I.T) Bombay. It is a unique blend of Surfactants, Lubricity Additive, Antioxidant, Cetane improver, Solvents & Combustion CatalySts etc. All the ingredients used in its preparation are organic in nature, thus making it safe for use in any industrial or domestic applications. .

  • Packing: 1000Ltrs, 200Ltrs, 20 Ltrs, 1 Ltrs, 500 ml, 250 ml, 100 ml, 50 ml & 25 ml.
  • Dosage: 1:2000 on Vol/Vol basis

THERMOL-P

A Multifunctional Combustion Catalyst For PETROL

Thermol-P is a multifunctional catalyst for Petrol, with a recommended dosage of only 25ml for every 50L of petrol. after Addition of THERMOL-P, deposits will be removed and won't appear later either, so the engine starts sooner, it won't shake as much as earlier when running, and it operates more smoothly. The vehicle will perform better and mileage will increase too (using the same amount of fuel)

  • Packing: 100 ml & 25 ml. Green in color.
  • Dosage: 1:2000 on wt/wt basis

THERMACT-PC

Optimizing petcoke combustion

THERMACT-PC is a fire side Combustion Catalyst developed to aid burning issues associated with Petcoke, aiming at reducing the Specific Fuel Consumption and reducing Emissions parallelly.

  • Packing: 20 kg Silsac bag
  • Dosage: 1 kg of THERMACT-PC is added to 7-10 tons of Pet Coke
